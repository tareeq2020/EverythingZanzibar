Our story

It started with a simple frustration.

Friends kept arriving in Zanzibar and leaving having seen only the inside of a resort. They'd miss the grandmother's kitchen, the unmarked beach, the dhow at golden hour — the island we actually live in.

So we built the friend we wished every visitor had: part insider guide, part event host, part concierge. One local team that plans your whole trip — from the airport pickup to the last party on the water.

Today that's guides, festivals, luxury yacht sailings, partner hotels and door-to-door transfers — all under one roof, all run by people who call this island home.
